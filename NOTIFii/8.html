    A while ago our neighbor went through something really traumatizing.
    Their 5 year old kid went missing since he went to the park to play. The parents couldn’t go to the
    nearest police station to report the missing case as such cases can only be reported after the first
    24hrs. His parents were getting helpless. Still the family dint leave any stone unturned. They went and
    searched through each n every nook of the area to find the kid. As it was getting dark, the situation got
    tougher for them. Luckily after 8 hrs of thorough search they were able to find that kid.

    Another similar case happened with an 86 year old grandma who was suffering from Alzheimer’s
    (memory loss) in the neighborhood. A fine afternoon her family was taking a rest after their lunch
    when their grandma suddenly went outside their home. Nobody knew she went out and to make things even worse, the family got to know about their grandma missing after the first 2 hours of the incident .In such cases where the patient even forgets their own identity for awhile it gets really tricky to track them back home.

    Then came the time when we thought about WHAT IF? there was an online notice board portal, very easy to access, with an extremely user friendly interface. People could simply login to that portal and could update about someone/something they lost or anything they want to get a lead of in/around the neighborhood. Its then available to general public which could later add/edit the notices with all necessary leads . People can also track the active/closed cases through the notice dashboard. This page makes SEARCHING easy by all means.
